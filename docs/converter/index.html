Ratezo Currency Converter

Keep track of currency rates with our smart Currency Converter app! Effortlessly check exchange rates between various currencies, and enjoy a seamless offline experience. Whether you are traveling, or tracking currency trends, our Currency Converter app is your ultimate companion for managing exchange rates anytime, anywhere.

Key Features

  • Currency rates: Get exchange rates for global currencies
  • Offline Support: Access your saved rates and calculate conversions offline without internet, ideal for international trips or limited connectivity
  • Manual Updates: Take control of your rates. Update them manually whenever you need
  • Customizable Themes: Personalize your app with various light and dark themes to suit your style
  • No Signups Required: Simply download and start using
  • Cloud Backups: Optionally enable cloud backups to e.g. Google Drive to save your settings, ensuring easy restoration during app reinstallations or phone changes

Exchange Rates

The exchange rates provided by the App are for informational purposes only and may not reflect the latest rates available. While we strive to provide exchange rate data that is refreshed within the last 24 hours, fluctuations in currency values and delays in updates can result in discrepancies. Please verify rates with your financial institution or preferred exchange service before making financial decisions or transactions. Use this app at your own discretion, and we are not liable for any discrepancies or financial losses incurred.
