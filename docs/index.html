EILA Money Manager

Expense Tracker and Budgets, Manage money like a Pro.

EILA is a smart and simple finance manager to keep track of Expenses, Income, Liabilities, Assets and Budgets.

With just a few clicks and taps you add an expense, income, liability, or an asset and let EILA take care of the rest, be it showing in various groupings, creating graphs/charts, or generating reports. With EILA you know where the money goes, what are your savings and assets, what do you owe others, means you are in full control of your finances, based on which you can plan for the future savings and retirement goals.

Key Features

  • No account or signups needed, just download the app and start using it
  • Manage finances in 4 main categories Expenses, Income, Liabilities, and Assets. Each main category has sub-categories e.g. Groceries, Salary, Health, House etc.
  • Periodic and rollover Budgets, including forecasting
  • Recurring Transactions
  • Loans and Loan Payments
  • Assets and Asset Sales
  • Currency converter
  • Organize transactions into accounts e.g. Bank Account, Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card, Prepaid Card etc
  • Password & Biometric Lock
  • Filter and display transactions by category and sub-category
  • Custom Transaction Categories
  • Hidden categories, for privacy
  • Hide amounts, for privacy
  • Beautiful graphs/charts
  • Enter transactions in almost any currency
  • Auto conversion from one currency transaction to the main currency. Note: internet access is needed to download currency rates
  • Transfer between accounts
  • Manual and Auto Backups, so you don’t lose data if the phone is damaged, lost or changed. Note, only if backups are enabled and backup was run on the device
  • Import/Migrate data from other similar Apps e.g. Monefy, Spendee, etc. Note, import is an experimental feature it might not be 100% accurate, it also depends on the files generated by EILA and other Apps
  • Export to CSV
  • Give it a personal touch by changing graphic colors
  • Dark and Light mode
  • You are in control of your data, the data is only saved on your device, it doesn’t reach our servers. Note, when you enable backups the backup is stored in your cloud service provider in your own account e.g. Google Drive
  • Simple to use
  • Your Local Language support. You can help us translate in your language
  • Your suggestions are most welcome

Disclaimer

This app does not provide financial services, personal finance products, or banking services of any kind. Additionally, nothing within the app should be considered financial advice. All data is either manually entered by you or imported from other apps or files.


We prioritize your privacy:

  • Your data is stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers
  • If you enable optional cloud backup, your data is securely stored in your chosen backup service, such as your Google Drive
